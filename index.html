Vaddeswaram is a town located in the state of Andhra Pradesh, India. It falls under the jurisdiction of Greater Visakhapatnam Agencies (GVA) and boasts a population of around 2.76 lakh. The weather in Vaddeswaram is typically hot and humid, with temperatures ranging from 27°C to 36°C throughout the year. The best time to visit Vaddeswaram is during the winter months from December to February when the temperatures drop, making it a refreshing time to explore the town and its surroundings.
